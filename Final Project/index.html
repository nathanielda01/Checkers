Checkers

Description

My project will be an online Checkers application. Checkers is a simple enough game with simple rules. On a Checkerboard, checkers of opposing colors are placed on the black squares. Two players take turns moving their respective checkers diagonally forward trying to take all of the opposing players checkers. To take an opposing player's checkers, with space available, a player jumps his/her checker over the opposing player's checker diagonally. The opposing player's checker is removed and the game continues. Another goal of both players is to progress their checkers across the board to the other side where they will then be promoted to Kings. A King may move and jump opposing pieces diagonally forward or backward.

There will be an 8X8 board array variable to coordinate where pieces need to go. There will be several methods to control where the pieces go. Furthermore, checker piece objects will be created to make data on pieces easier to access.

CheckerPiece There will be checker piece objects that will hold color data and a bool to determine if it should be made a king or not.

Game The Game object will contain properties that will determine whose move it is, what pieces can be moved, winning conditions, etc.
